Poor men walk while rich men ride\
**Wake up below there's a monkey in the hold**\
And we poor sailors are waiting on the tide\
**Wake up below there's a monkey in the hold**

>**Wake up, wake up, the world's growing old\
There's a donkey in the wheelhouse\
There's a monkey in the hold**

We're bound for Hell's Mouth, so I've been told\
**Wake up below there's a monkey in the hold**\
Where the poor shovel shit and the rich count the gold\
**Wake up below there's a monkey in the hold**

>**Wake up, wake up...**

The wind it is a-blowing and the ship it is a-pitching\
**Wake up below there's a monkey in the hold**\
If you growl too much, they'll send you to Hell's Kitchen\
**Wake up below there's a monkey in the hold**

>**Wake up, wake up...**

The Captain's in the treasury he's eating all the gold\
**Wake up below there's a monkey in the hold**\
In between the decks, folks is hungry and they're cold\
**Wake up below there's a monkey in the hold**

>**Wake up, wake up, the world's growing old\
There's a donkey in the wheelhouse\
There's a monkey in the hold**

If you fall behind when the ship is making speed\
**Wake up below there's a monkey in the hold**\
The sharks'll have your knackers as they can't control their greed\
**Wake up below there's a monkey in the hold**

>**Wake up, wake up...**

The officers commanding us, they cheer and raise their glasses\
**Wake up below there's a monkey in the hold**\
But the higher up the mast they climb, the more we see they're [their?] arses\
**Wake up below there's a monkey in the hold**

>**Wake up, wake up...**

We poor sailors are a-struggling on the tide\
**Wake up below there's a monkey in the hold**\
For the compass is a-missing and the world is on its side\
**Wake up below there's a monkey in the hold**
