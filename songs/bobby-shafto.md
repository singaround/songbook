Bobby Shaftoe’s gone to sea,\
With silver buckles on his knee:\
He’ll come back and marry me,\
Bonny Bobby Shaftoe!

Bobby Shaftoe’s fat and fair,\
Combing down his yellow hair;\
He’s my love for evermore,\
Bonny Bobby Shaftoe.
